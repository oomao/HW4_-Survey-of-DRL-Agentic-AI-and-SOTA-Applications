## 2. Ablation Study (flagship query)

Query: *"Survey robotics VLA models in 2024-2026"*

| Configuration | Sub-topics | Papers found | Coverage F1 | Tool calls | Critic rounds |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Full (plan+critic) | 3 | 6 | 1.00 | 22 | 1 |
| − Critic | 3 | 5 | 0.91 | 18 | 0 |
| − Planner (single ReAct) | 1 | 4 | 0.80 | 13 | 0 |

**Reading the ablation:**
- Removing the **Critic** drops papers found from 6 to 5 and F1 from 1.00 to 0.91: without the coverage loop, the seminal Diffusion Policy paper (pre-2024) is never recovered after cross-topic dedup empties its sub-topic.
- Removing the **Planner** collapses 3 sub-topics to 1; F1 falls to 0.80 because a single ReAct loop cannot cover the breadth a decomposed plan does.
